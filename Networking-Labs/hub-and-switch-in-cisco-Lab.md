# Star Topology Lab - Cisco Packet Tracer

## Objective
To understand how star topology works using a switch and hub.

---

## What is Star Topology?

Star topology is a network design where all devices are connected to a central device such as a switch or hub.

---

## Devices Used

- 1 Switch
- 4 PCs
- Copper straight-through cables

---

## Network Diagram

![Star Topology](images/star-topology.png)

---

## IP Configuration

PC1: 192.168.1.1  
PC2: 192.168.1.2  
PC3: 192.168.1.3  
PC4: 192.168.1.4  

Subnet Mask: 255.255.255.0

---

## What I Observed

- Switch forwards data intelligently.
- Hub broadcasts data to all devices.
- Star topology is easy to manage.
- If switch fails, whole network goes down.

---

## Difference Between Hub and Switch

| Hub | Switch |
|------|--------|
| Broadcasts to all | Sends to specific device |
| Slower | Faster |
| Less secure | More secure |
